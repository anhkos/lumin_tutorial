Bright dune:	A type of sand dune found on Mars.
Crater:	Images of craters with specific diameter and visible rims.
Dark dune:	Dunes that are completely defrosted on Mars.
Other:	A catch-all class for images that don't fit the defined classes.
Slope streak:	Features formed by dark flow-like features on slopes.
Impact ejecta:	Evidence of meteorite impact on Mars' surface.
Spider:	A phenomenon in the south polar region of Mars with a central pit and radial troughs.
Swiss cheese:	Terrain with pits formed by sublimation of ice.
